ABOUT US

Grama Bharathi was established in 1996 as an NGO with the sole objective off making Indian villages self-sufficient vibrant while preserving natural resources.

Grama Bharati has several aims and objectives which together would help realize in making the Indian villages independent and self-reliant. The NGO was started by few dedicated social workers and quickly grew into a state wide dedicated volunteer social workers group. These volunteers continue to grow and operate.
